## GOS-M05 — Growth Readiness Levels

**[GOS-M05]** Communities and the statewide platform progress through **six readiness levels**:

### Level 1 — Discoverable

Communities can be found · public profiles exist [PCN-001] · invitations function [ICS-001] · basic participation possible

### Level 2 — Welcoming

New participants experience belonging [WBS-001] · mentors exist · volunteer opportunities exist [OEX-001] · communities actively welcome newcomers

### Level 3 — Growing

Communities recruit sustainably · leadership develops [CLD-001] · representation expands [CGO-001] · knowledge is shared [CKLS-001]

### Level 4 — Reproducing

Existing communities **successfully launch and mentor new communities** [CEF-001 Foundry] · expansion becomes **repeatable**

### Level 5 — Statewide Network

Every county · every college · every trade school · future high schools · institutional partners [IPS-001] · communities collaborate statewide [SCN-001] · network interconnected [NISS-M16 Network Twin]

### Level 6 — Self-Expanding Ecosystem

Growth **no longer founder-dependent**. Communities continuously: develop leaders · launch new communities · preserve knowledge · strengthen one another · share capabilities · welcome new participants. **Ecosystem grows through relationships rather than centralized control.**

**[GOS-M05a]** Progressive readiness — not binary pass/fail [AOS-M05 pattern].

**[GOS-M05b]** Orchestrator: `getGrowthReadinessLevel(communityId | 'statewide')`.

**[GOS-M05c]** Route: `/growth/readiness` · **Network Readiness Dashboard** [GOS-M08].

# Training Requirements Model — CAL-P2 Wave 2A

13 training keys in `data/calendar/staffing-training-catalog.json`.

## Completion modes

- `self_attested` — labeled as self-attested
- `manager_verified` — requires verifying manager
- `system_recorded` — future durable records
- `on_site_briefing` — only where configured

## Rules

- Missing training may block confirmation later (Wave 2B)
- Interest may be allowed before training unless role forbids it
- Expired training does not count as current

# Shift Leadership Model — CAL-P2 Wave 2A

Shift leads are distinct from event owners.

## Assignment states

`suggested` → `invited` → `accepted` | `declined` | `removed`

## Rules

- Lead requirement not satisfied by suggested lead alone
- Accepted lead is soft-beta while Gate A is open
- Primary, co-lead, and backup roles are distinguishable
- Candidate-private briefing content remains restricted

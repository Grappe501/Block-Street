# Launch Tracks A–J — Product Shipping Map

**Status:** Frozen for Version 1.0 Certification  
**Updated:** 2026-07-14

These tracks are the product surface map used for Pass 1 capability audits.  
They are **not** Living Intelligence wave numbers. Protocol completeness does not equal track completeness.

---

## Track A — Platform Foundation

Auth session model, data/store boundaries, API coherence, env/deploy, observability.

## Track B — Identity, Invitation, LocalBrain, and Onboarding

Invitation → belonging → LocalBrain → first productive action.  
**Key metric:** ≤10 minutes invitation → productive.

## Track C — Daily Workspace and Executive Experience

Daily briefing, Today, calendar, priorities, meeting prep — prefer platform over inbox.

## Track D — Collaboration, Teams, and Human Coordination

Join and contribute without someone explaining the product.

## Track E — Knowledge, Memory, AI, and Organizational Intelligence

Knowledge survives leadership change; LocalBrain + institutional intelligence.

## Track F — Missions, Projects, Operations, and Execution

Strategy → completed work with visibility.

## Track G — Communications, Engagement, and Relationship Intelligence

Conversations, meetings memory, notifications, relationship context.

## Track H — Administration, Governance, and Organization Management

Day-to-day admin without developers.

## Track I — Quality, Security, Reliability, and Production Hardening

“Would we run our own organization on this?”

## Track J — Beta, Adoption, Launch, and Continuous Growth

New organization succeeds without the founding team.

---

## Certification order (after Pass 1 gap ranking)

Suggested **fix / prove** order (not invent new architecture):

1. **B** first vertical slice (identity + LocalBrain product presence + belonging)
2. **C** executive daily OS
3. **D** collaboration
4. **E** knowledge survival
5. **F** missions/ops
6. **G** communications polish & unification
7. **H** admin without engineers
8. **I** harden what we ship
9. **J** prove a new org can succeed
10. **A** close remaining foundation gaps exposed by other tracks

Pass 1 may reorder this once the matrix is authoritative.

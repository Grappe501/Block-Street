## Phase 6 — Growth Operating System (Active)



**Question:** *How does the platform grow itself?*



**Not marketing. Not recruiting.** Organic community expansion through relationships.



**Growth loop:** Relationships → Communities → Action → Stories → New People → Relationships



**Signature architecture:** **Living Network Graph [GOS-M16]** — unifies Relationship, Trust, Growth, Conversation, Capability, Decision, Improvement, Operational, and Impact graphs into the platform's master self-model.



Growth Constitution · Personal Organizing Network · Community Growth · Ambassadors · Onboarding · Growth Intelligence · Invitations · Recognition · Community Expansion · Partnerships · Alumni Network · Public Presence · Network Intelligence · Certification

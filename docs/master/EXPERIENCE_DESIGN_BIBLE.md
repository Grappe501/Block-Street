## EDB-M01 — Purpose

**[EDB-M01]** Ensure every interaction **reinforces culture and values** — reducing anxiety, building confidence, celebrating progress, earning trust over months and years.

**[EDB-M01a]** Evaluation question [PEL-M13]: *Does this strengthen relationships, deepen belonging, and help people grow into community builders?*

## Persona Experience Arcs [EDB-C01]

### First-time visitor [EDB-C01a]

**Should feel:** Curious · welcomed · not sold to · local · possible.

**First 60 seconds:** Clear value · Arkansas-rooted · see real communities · one low-friction explore action [PCN-001].

**Avoid:** Signup walls · jargon · empty statewide map · corporate tone.

### New volunteer [EDB-C01b]

**Should feel:** Needed · prepared · connected · not overwhelmed.

**First session:** Welcome companion [WBS-001] · named contact · one meaningful task · thank-you within 24 hours [CRA-001].

### New community leader [EDB-C01c]

**Should feel:** Supported · not alone · empowered · responsible.

**First week:** Foundry wizard [CEF-001] · mentor connection [CLD-001] · charter clarity [CCN-001] · first mission win.

### Lifelong member [EDB-C01d]

**Should feel:** Legacy · contribution visible · open door for return [LCN-001 · CP-020].

## The First Five Minutes [EDB-C02]

After registration [USR-001 → PHQ-001]:

| Minute | Experience goal | System support |
|--------|-----------------|----------------|
| 0–1 | "I'm in the right place" | Personalized welcome · home community named |
| 1–2 | "I know what to do next" | One primary action · Morning Brief preview |
| 2–3 | "People like me are here" | Social proof · stories · invite context |
| 3–4 | "My privacy is respected" | Trust center link · visibility explained [TPS-001] |
| 4–5 | "I can grow here" | Journey stage intro · opportunity teaser [OBE-001] |

## After First Mission [EDB-C03]

**[EDB-C03a]** **Celebrate:** Story prompt · gratitude from community · impact visible [CIIS-001 · CST-001].

**[EDB-C03b]** **Reflect:** Optional short reflection [LIS-001] — not mandatory homework.

**[EDB-C03c]** **Connect:** Suggest one relationship strengthen action [RGE-001].

**[EDB-C03d]** **Invite:** Only after positive experience — never in first session [GCN-M03 Belonging Before Growth].

---

## Emotional Design Principles [EDB-C04]

1. **Reduce anxiety** — always show where you are · what's expected · who to ask
2. **Create confidence** — small wins early · progress visible · skills acknowledged [PGL-001]
3. **Celebrate progress** — appreciation not gamification [CRA-001]
4. **Build trust slowly** — transparency · consent · no surprise visibility changes [TPS-001]
5. **Honor culture** — community voice in copy · local names · stories over statistics [CST-001]
6. **Belonging before metrics** — never optimize engagement at cost of belonging [GCN-M03]

## Trust Over Time [EDB-C05]

| Period | Trust goal | Mechanisms |
|--------|------------|------------|
| Day 1 | Safety | Privacy defaults · clear policies |
| Week 1 | Reliability | Consistent welcome · responsive moderators |
| Month 1 | Contribution | Mission completed · named in thanks |
| Year 1 | Leadership | Mentorship · succession visible [CLD-001] |
| Years | Legacy | Stories preserved · alumni pathway [CLS-001 · LCN-001] |

## Anxiety Reduction Patterns [EDB-C06]

- **Ambiguity:** Always show status of applications, invitations, missions
- **Isolation:** Surface "others from your campus" · buddy suggestions
- **Overwhelm:** Progressive disclosure · "just one thing today" [CAM-001 attention budget]
- **Rejection:** Kind language on declines · alternative opportunities [OBE-001]
- **Comparison:** No public leaderboards · gratitude board not ranking [CRA-001]

---

## Celebration & Gratitude [EDB-C07]

**[EDB-C07a]** Milestones trigger **Community Gratitude** moments — not points [CRA-M01].

**[EDB-C07b]** Celebrations are **story-based** — who helped · what changed · why it mattered.

**[EDB-C07c]** Leaders celebrate **others first** — service before recognition [MAB principle 5].

---

## Copy & Tone [EDB-C08]

**Voice:** Warm · direct · youth-accessible · Arkansas-proud · never patronizing.

**Avoid:** "Users" (say participants) · "Platform" when community fits · engagement bait · urgency manipulation.

**Inclusive:** Gender-neutral default · accessible reading level · translation-ready (future).

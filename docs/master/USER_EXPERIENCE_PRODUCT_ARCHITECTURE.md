## UXB-M01 — Purpose

**[UXB-M01]** Volume 4 defines **how humans experience the platform** — experience principles, navigation, workspaces, dashboards, journeys, design language, components, collaboration, mobile, AI presentation, engagement, trust, and institutional tailoring.

**[UXB-M01a]** This volume establishes the complete experience architecture **before Burt writes production UI**.

**[UXB-M01b]** Volume 3 defines **how the platform behaves**; Volume 4 defines **how participants experience that behavior**.

## Guiding Principle

**[UXB-M02]**

> **Experience should feel intentional, welcoming, and trustworthy.**

Every screen, journey, and interaction should reinforce belonging and institutional confidence.

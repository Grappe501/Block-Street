## Purpose

**[UXB-AIX01]** The AI Experience Architecture defines how artificial intelligence is **experienced** throughout the Community Operating System.

**[UXB-AIX01a]** AI is **not** a feature. AI is **not** a chatbot.

**[UXB-AIX01b]** AI is a **distributed operational capability** that quietly assists participants everywhere they work.

**[UXB-AIX01c]** Participants should never feel like they are "using AI." They should feel like they have an **exceptionally knowledgeable teammate** who understands their work, remembers context, explains itself, and helps them accomplish meaningful goals.

---

## Guiding Principle

**[UXB-AIX02]**

> **AI should amplify human judgment, never replace it.**

**[UXB-AIX02a]** The platform exists to build **stronger communities** — not dependency on artificial intelligence.

---

## Philosophy

**[UXB-AIX03]** Traditional AI experiences begin with: *"Ask me anything."*

**[UXB-AIX03a]** The Community Operating System begins with: *"How can I help you accomplish your mission today?"*

**[UXB-AIX03b]** **AI is contextual rather than generic.**

---

## AI Experience Architecture

**[UXB-AIX04]** Every AI interaction follows the same operational model:

```text
Context
      ↓
Understanding
      ↓
Knowledge Retrieval
      ↓
Reasoning
      ↓
Guidance
      ↓
Human Decision
      ↓
Knowledge Capture
```

**[UXB-AIX04a]** **AI participates in work rather than interrupting it.**

---

## AI Principles

**[UXB-AIX05]** Every AI interaction should be:

Context-aware · Transparent · Grounded · Explainable · Collaborative · Permission-aware · Privacy-respecting · Advisory

**[UXB-AIX05a]** **The participant always remains in control.**

---

## AI Roles

**[UXB-AIX06]** Rather than one assistant, the platform contains a **federation of specialized AI roles**:

### Personal Coach

**Helps:** Organize work · Understand priorities · Manage time · Track goals · Reflect · Grow

**Understands the Personal Operating System.**

### Community Guide

**Helps:** Community history · Members · Traditions · Events · Community Health · Operating Manual

**Becomes the institutional memory for each community.**

### Mission Planner

**Helps:** Plan missions · Estimate resources · Build timelines · Identify risks · Recommend volunteers · Suggest playbooks

**Understands operational execution** [PBA-006].

### Volunteer Coach

**Helps:** Find opportunities · Recommend training · Track growth · Suggest mentors · Celebrate milestones · Support volunteer wellness

**Encourages long-term participation** [PBA-007].

### Leadership Advisor

**Helps:** Develop leaders · Suggest mentorship · Identify succession opportunities · Summarize leadership health · Recommend training

**Leadership decisions remain human** [PBA-008].

### Knowledge Navigator

**Helps:** Search Community Brain · Summarize research · Locate stories · Recommend playbooks · Identify related lessons

**Knowledge becomes conversational** [PBA-013].

### Executive Advisor

**Helps:** Summarize Operational Intelligence · Prepare briefings · Explain trends · Highlight risks · Compare communities · Suggest strategic questions

**AI supports executive thinking** [PBA-014].

### Governance Assistant

**Helps:** Summarize proposals · Compare historical precedent · Explain policies · Draft governance documents · Identify conflicts

**Governance remains transparent** [PBA-012].

### Research Assistant

**Helps:** Organize research · Summarize sources · Compare documents · Suggest additional reading · Generate citations

**Research becomes faster.**

### Writing Assistant

**Helps:** Draft communications · Improve writing · Generate summaries · Translate knowledge · Adapt tone

**Writing remains participant-owned.**

## Major Architectural Recommendation: AI Federation

**[UXB-AIX20]** Replace the concept of a single platform assistant with an **AI Federation**.

**[UXB-AIX20a]** The AI Federation is a coordinated network of specialized AI agents sharing constitutional principles, operational context, and knowledge foundation — each developing **deep expertise in a particular domain**.

### Executive AI Council

**[UXB-AIX20b]** Coordinates:

Operational priorities · Context routing · Cross-agent collaboration · Governance compliance · Resource allocation

**The participant experiences one seamless AI, while specialized agents collaborate behind the scenes.**

### Domain AI Specialists

**[UXB-AIX20c]** Eight domain intelligence agents:

| Agent | Domain |
|-------|--------|
| **Community Intelligence Agent** | Community Health · Growth · Relationships · Community Operating Manual |
| **Mission Intelligence Agent** | Mission planning · Logistics · Reflection · Resource optimization |
| **Leadership Intelligence Agent** | Leadership development · Mentorship · Succession · Leadership Academy |
| **Volunteer Intelligence Agent** | Volunteer Success Center · Recognition · Matching · Wellness |
| **Knowledge Intelligence Agent** | Community Brain · Knowledge Observatory · Playbooks · Stories · Research |
| **Governance Intelligence Agent** | Constitutional interpretation · Policies · Decisions · Appeals · Transparency |
| **Executive Intelligence Agent** | Executive Operations Center · Strategic forecasting · Operational Intelligence · Executive briefings |
| **Communications Intelligence Agent** | Writing · Public messaging · Meeting preparation · Community communication · Knowledge translation |

### Shared Operational Memory

**[UXB-AIX20d]** Every agent shares access (subject to permissions) to:

Community Knowledge Graph · Community Event Ledger · Digital Twins · Business Rules · Workflows · Calendars · Knowledge Observatory · Community Brain

**Participants never have to repeat context.**

### Agent Collaboration

**[UXB-AIX20e]** Example — a leader asks: *"Help me prepare for tomorrow's county volunteer summit."*

The system may automatically coordinate:

- Executive Intelligence Agent → summarize county conditions
- Volunteer Intelligence Agent → review volunteer participation
- Mission Intelligence Agent → prepare mission updates
- Knowledge Intelligence Agent → retrieve relevant playbooks
- Communications Intelligence Agent → draft opening remarks
- Calendar Intelligence → verify schedule and logistics

**The participant receives one integrated response, even though multiple agents collaborated.**

### Human-Centered Coordination

**[UXB-AIX20f]** The participant **never manages the federation**. They describe what they are trying to accomplish.

The AI Federation determines: Which specialists are needed · Which knowledge should be retrieved · Which recommendations are appropriate · Which approvals are required · Which uncertainties should be highlighted

**[UXB-AIX20g]** Architectural evolution:

Rather than embedding isolated AI features, the COS gains a **coordinated intelligence layer** that understands the participant, their communities, missions, knowledge, leadership responsibilities, and operational context.

**[UXB-AIX20h]** Constitutional property:

> **AI is not an independent authority. It is a constitutional public servant within the Community Operating System — working alongside people to strengthen communities while remaining transparent, explainable, and accountable.**

## Burt Implementation Guidance

**[UXB-AIX21]** Implementation should:

1. Build AI as **distributed contextual assistants**
2. **Automatically assemble context** before prompting
3. Make every recommendation **explainable**
4. **Require citations** where applicable
5. Integrate **Community Brain, Digital Twins, Community Event Ledger, Operational Intelligence, and Knowledge Growth Engine**
6. **Preserve human authority** over every consequential decision
7. Consult **AI Federation** spec before AI-facing features

**[UXB-AIX21a]** Logical home: Experience Architecture schema — AIRole · AIContext · ExplainabilityModel · AIFederation · ExecutiveAICouncil.

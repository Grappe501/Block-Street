## Purpose

**[UXB-WSA01]** The Workspace Architecture defines how every participant performs **meaningful work** inside the Community Operating System.

**[UXB-WSA01a]** A workspace is **not** a page. A workspace is a **living operational environment** where people, communities, knowledge, calendars, workflows, AI, and operational intelligence come together around a shared purpose.

**[UXB-WSA01b]** Participants should feel like they are **entering a place where meaningful work happens** — not opening another web application.

---

## Guiding Principle

**[UXB-WSA02]**

> **People work in environments, not pages.**

**[UXB-WSA02a]** Every workspace should feel like a **complete operational home**.

---

## Philosophy

**[UXB-WSA03]** Traditional software organizes work around modules:

CRM → Calendar → Tasks → Reports → Settings

**[UXB-WSA03a]** The Community Operating System organizes work around **missions**:

Build Communities · Develop Leaders · Coordinate Missions · Grow Knowledge · Support Volunteers · Strengthen Partnerships

**[UXB-WSA03b]** **Everything begins with purpose** [PBA-001].

---

**[UXB-WSA04]** Every workspace follows the same structure:

```text

      ↓
```

**[UXB-WSA04a]** **Every workspace shares the same rhythm** [UXB-DAW05].

## Universal Workspace Framework

**[UXB-WSA06]** Every workspace contains eight zones:

### Mission Zone

**[UXB-WSA06a]** Always visible. Answers: *Why am I here?* · *What are we trying to accomplish?* · *What matters most today?*

**[UXB-WSA06b]** **Mission anchors the workspace.**

### People Zone

**[UXB-WSA06c]** Displays: Communities · Teams · Volunteers · Mentors · Leaders · Partners

**[UXB-WSA06d]** **Work is always connected to people.**

### Work Zone

**[UXB-WSA06e]** Shows: Active workflows · Tasks · Approvals · Current missions · Open decisions

**[UXB-WSA06f]** **Current work remains central.**

### Knowledge Zone

**[UXB-WSA06g]** Displays: Community Brain · Playbooks · Stories · Lessons · Research · Historical guidance

**[UXB-WSA06h]** **Knowledge accompanies every activity** [PBA-013].

### Calendar Zone

**[UXB-WSA06i]** Shows: Meetings · Volunteer commitments · Deadlines · Mission milestones · Travel

**[UXB-WSA06j]** **Time becomes operational context.**

### Intelligence Zone

**[UXB-WSA06k]** Displays: Community Intelligence · Operational Intelligence · Digital Twins · AI recommendations · Community Health

**[UXB-WSA06l]** **Intelligence supports work** [PBA-009 · PBA-014].

### History Zone

**[UXB-WSA06m]** Displays: Community Event Ledger · Timeline · Recent changes · Governance history · Mission history

**[UXB-WSA06n]** **History provides continuity** [DAB-007].

### Reflection Zone

**[UXB-WSA06o]** Encourages: Lessons learned · Mission reflections · Community feedback · Recognition · Growth planning

**[UXB-WSA06p]** **Reflection closes every operational cycle.**

## Workspace Types

**[UXB-WSA07]** Nine primary workspace types govern operational environments:

### Personal Workspace

**[UXB-WSA07a]** **Purpose:** Organize one participant's life inside the platform.

**Includes:** Personal OS · Calendar · Communities · Volunteer work · Learning · Goals · Digital Twin

### Community Workspace

**[UXB-WSA07b]** **Purpose:** Operate one community.

**Includes:** Members · Leadership · Knowledge · Calendar · Missions · Community Health · Community Operating Manual

### Mission Workspace

**[UXB-WSA07c]** **Purpose:** Coordinate mission execution.

**Includes:** Mission Operations Center · Timeline · Volunteers · Knowledge · Logistics · Reflection · Impact

### Leadership Workspace

**[UXB-WSA07d]** **Purpose:** Develop community leaders.

**Includes:** Leadership Academy · Mentorship · Pipeline · Succession · Training · Leadership Health

### Volunteer Workspace

**[UXB-WSA07e]** **Purpose:** Support volunteer growth.

**Includes:** Volunteer Success Center · Assignments · Training · Recognition · Communities · Mentorship

### County Workspace

**[UXB-WSA07f]** **Purpose:** Coordinate regional operations.

**Includes:** Communities · Institutions · Leadership · Coverage · Growth · Operational Intelligence · County Digital Twin

### Institution Workspace

**[UXB-WSA07g]** **Purpose:** Support schools, nonprofits, churches, campaigns, civic groups, and partner organizations.

**Includes:** Organization profile · Members · Calendar · Knowledge · Communities · Leadership · Partnerships · Institution Operating Manual

### Knowledge Workspace

**[UXB-WSA07h]** **Purpose:** Grow institutional intelligence.

**Includes:** Knowledge Observatory · Community Brain · Playbooks · Stories · Research · Innovation · Legacy

### Executive Workspace

**[UXB-WSA07i]** **Purpose:** Strategic leadership.

**Includes:** Executive Operations Center · Community Health · Forecasts · Operational readiness · Risks · Executive briefings · Platform Digital Twin

## Major Architectural Recommendation: Unified Workspace Manager

**[UXB-WSA17]** Create a **Unified Workspace Manager (UWM)** as the **operating shell** for the entire Community Operating System.

**[UXB-WSA17a]** Rather than treating workspaces as isolated destinations, the UWM continuously coordinates them into **one seamless operational environment**.

**[UXB-WSA17b]** The UWM manages six coordination domains:

### Workspace Context

Current workspace · Current mission · Current community · Current institution · Current county · Current role

### Workspace State

Open panels · Active workflows · Search state · Calendar state · Selected people · AI conversation context

### Cross-Workspace Coordination

**[UXB-WSA17c]** Example: A volunteer working inside a Mission Workspace can — without losing context:

- Open the Community Workspace
- Review the Community Operating Manual
- Enter the Leadership Workspace
- Schedule meetings in Calendar
- Capture lessons in the Knowledge Workspace
- Return to the original mission exactly where they left it

**No re-navigation is required.**

### Operational Memory

**[UXB-WSA17d]** The manager remembers:

Frequently used workspaces · Open missions · Active approvals · Draft work · Recent collaborators · Workspace layouts

**Participants resume work naturally after interruptions.**

### AI Coordination

**[UXB-WSA17e]** Because the UWM understands every active context, AI can respond naturally:

- *"Take me back to yesterday's mission."*
- *"Show me everything related to Pine Bluff."*
- *"Compare this community with Jonesboro."*
- *"Open the leadership notes from last week."*
- *"Continue where I left off."*

**AI interacts with the participant's operational environment — not isolated pages.**

### Executive View

**[UXB-WSA17f]** For leaders managing multiple organizations simultaneously:

Multiple active workspace sessions · Shared calendars · Cross-community mission visibility · Unified notification management · Organization-wide search · Global AI context

**[UXB-WSA17g]** Architectural vision:

> **The Community Operating System should feel less like a website and more like a living operating environment for communities, leaders, and institutions.**

**[UXB-WSA17h]** The UWM extends the Adaptive Workspace Engine [UXB-DAW17] from dashboard composition to **full operational shell coordination**.

## Burt Implementation Guidance

**[UXB-WSA18]** Implementation should:

1. Treat workspaces as **operational environments** rather than pages
2. Maintain the **universal workspace framework**
3. **Preserve context** across transitions
4. Integrate **AI, Digital Twins, Community Event Ledger, and Community Intelligence**
5. Support **multi-workspace participation**
6. Build **mobile-first** with **accessibility by default**
7. Consult **Unified Workspace Manager** spec before workspace-facing features

**[UXB-WSA18a]** Logical home: Experience Architecture schema — WorkspaceZone · WorkspaceType · WorkspaceContext · UnifiedWorkspaceManager.

## Purpose

**[PBA-KGE01]** The Knowledge Growth Engine defines how **experience becomes knowledge**, how **knowledge becomes wisdom**, and how **wisdom becomes institutional memory** throughout the Community Operating System.

**[PBA-KGE01a]** Communities should **never have to solve the same problem twice**.

**[PBA-KGE01b]** Every mission, meeting, conversation, success, failure, experiment, and story should strengthen the knowledge available to every future participant.

**[PBA-KGE01c]** **Knowledge is one of the platform's primary products.**

---

## Guiding Principle

**[PBA-KGE02]**

> **Every experience should leave behind reusable knowledge.**

**[PBA-KGE02a]** The value of the platform **compounds as communities learn from one another**.

---

## Philosophy

**[PBA-KGE03]** Traditional systems store documents. The Community Operating System cultivates **living knowledge**.

**[PBA-KGE03a]** Rather than asking *Where is the document?* the platform asks:

- What did we learn?
- Who needs this knowledge?
- Which communities can reuse it?
- What changed because of this experience?
- What should future leaders know?

**[PBA-KGE03b]** **Knowledge becomes an active operational asset.**

---

## Knowledge Growth Lifecycle

**[PBA-KGE04]** Every piece of knowledge follows a lifecycle:

```text
Experience
      ↓
Capture
      ↓
Reflection
      ↓
Validation
      ↓
Publication
      ↓
Discovery
      ↓
Reuse
      ↓
Improvement
      ↓
Legacy
```

**[PBA-KGE04a]** **Knowledge should continuously evolve.**

## Organizational Learning Loop

**[PBA-KGE27]** With the Knowledge Growth Engine in place, the architecture supports a full learning loop:

1. Communities perform missions
2. Missions generate experiences
3. Experiences become structured knowledge
4. Knowledge improves future missions
5. Future missions generate better experiences

**[PBA-KGE27a]** This creates a **compounding cycle of organizational learning** — every generation inherits a stronger, wiser, better-documented community.

## Major Architectural Recommendation: Knowledge Observatory

**[PBA-KGE28]** Introduce a **Knowledge Observatory (KO)** as the platform's central environment for institutional learning.

**[PBA-KGE28a]** The Knowledge Observatory is **not simply a document library** — it continuously monitors how knowledge is created, shared, reused, improved, and preserved across the entire Community Operating System.

**[PBA-KGE28b]** The Knowledge Observatory integrates seven domains:

| Domain | Monitors |
|--------|----------|
| **Knowledge Creation** | Stories · Lessons · Playbooks · Community Brain entries · Research · Mission reflections |
| **Knowledge Quality** | Validation status · Evidence strength · Review history · Freshness · Community feedback |
| **Knowledge Reuse** | Communities reusing playbooks · Missions citing lessons · Leadership guidance referenced · Training materials adopted |
| **Knowledge Gaps** | Frequently asked unanswered questions · Missing playbooks · Outdated guidance · Undocumented mission types |
| **Learning** | Leadership Academy content · Volunteer training · Community onboarding · Recommended learning paths |
| **Intelligence** | Emerging best practices · Repeating problems · Successful innovations · Cross-community insights · AI-assisted synthesis |
| **Legacy** | Historical archives · Community traditions · Leadership histories · Institutional milestones |

**[PBA-KGE28c]** The Knowledge Observatory allows leaders to answer:

- What are our most valuable playbooks?
- Which communities are creating the most reusable knowledge?
- What lessons are being ignored?
- Where do we repeatedly encounter the same problems?
- What knowledge should every new community receive?

**[PBA-KGE28d]** The platform is no longer simply executing work — it is continuously **learning from its own experience**.

**[PBA-KGE28e]** Live spec: `data/registry/knowledge-growth-engine.json` · `knowledgeObservatory`

## Burt Implementation Guidance

**[PBA-KGE29]** Implementation should:

1. Treat knowledge as a **living operational asset**
2. Require **structured reflection before publication**
3. Preserve **version history**
4. Track **reuse and impact**
5. Integrate with **Community Brain, Digital Twins, Community Knowledge Graph, and AI**
6. Keep knowledge **human-governed** [PBA-012]
7. Consult **Knowledge Observatory** spec before knowledge-facing features

**[PBA-KGE29a]** Logical home: Platform Behavior schema — KnowledgeObject · KnowledgeCapture · KnowledgeValidation · KnowledgePublication · KnowledgeReuse · KnowledgeObservatory.

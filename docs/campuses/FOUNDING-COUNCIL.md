# Founding Leadership Council

## Purpose

Five Arkansas campuses anchor the initial leadership council. They are **founding partners**, not bosses over other schools.

## Members

| Campus | Slug | City |
|--------|------|------|
| University of Arkansas — Fayetteville | `ua-fayetteville` | Fayetteville |
| Ouachita Baptist University | `ouachita-baptist` | Arkadelphia |
| Philander Smith University | `philander-smith` | Little Rock |
| University of Central Arkansas | `uca` | Conway |
| University of Arkansas at Pine Bluff | `ua-pine-bluff` | Pine Bluff |

## Council Responsibilities (Planned)

- Represent campus diversity in platform governance
- Help onboard additional Arkansas schools
- Provide feedback on feature priorities
- Model relational organizing on their campuses
- Connect with county hubs in their regions

## What Council Does NOT Do

- Control other campuses' organizing priorities
- Endorse candidates on behalf of the platform
- Gatekeep which schools can join
- Override student decisions at other campuses

## On the Website

- `/council` — founding council page with campus profiles
- Each campus flagged `is_founding_council: true` in data
- Council section on homepage

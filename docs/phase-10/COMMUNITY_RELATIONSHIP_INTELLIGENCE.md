# Community Relationship Intelligence Engine

**System ID:** REL-001 · Build 10.3 · AC-196

Communities grow stronger when relationships become healthier, more diverse, and more resilient—not when individuals become more monitored.

## Capabilities

- Canonical relationship graph (nodes, edges, events)
- Relationship ledger with verification and explainable strength
- Mentorship graph with load analytics
- Collaboration and partnership intelligence
- Community connector identification
- Network resilience and isolation detection
- Privacy-aware graph permissions
- Federation aggregated analytics only
- AI relationship advisor (advisory only)
- User, organization, county, and federation dashboards

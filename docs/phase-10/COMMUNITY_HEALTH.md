# Community Health & Civic Resilience Engine

**System ID:** CHD-001 · Build 10.4 · AC-197

Healthy communities are defined by resilient relationships, sustained participation, capable leadership, trusted institutions, and the ability to solve problems together.

## Capabilities

- Canonical Community Health Profile with explainable Health Index
- Civic resilience framework with 6 resilience states
- Capacity analytics (volunteer, leadership, organizational readiness)
- Organizational ecosystem health measurement
- Community risk detection and opportunity mapping
- Geographic health maps (neighborhood, city, county, region)
- Longitudinal trend tracking and anonymous benchmarking
- AI community advisor (advisory only)
- Aggregated community reports and federation analytics

**Engine:** `src/lib/community-health/` · **Registry:** `data/registry/community-health.json`  
**Docs:** `docs/platform/community-health/`

# Civic Outcomes & Impact Measurement Engine

**System ID:** OUT-001 · Build 10.5 · AC-198

Measure real-world impact, not platform activity.

## Capabilities

- Canonical outcomes model distinguishing outputs, outcomes, and impact
- Outcomes ledger with baseline measurement and verified evidence
- Theory of Change framework per program
- Explainable attribution (correlation → contribution → likely → direct)
- Program evaluation and longitudinal tracking
- Civic Return on Participation (CROP) and advisory CROI
- AI impact advisor (advisory only, never fabricates evidence)
- Privacy-aware impact reports and federation aggregates

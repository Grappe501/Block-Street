# Civic Strategic Intelligence & Decision Support Engine

**System ID:** INT-002 · Build 10.6 · AC-199

The platform informs decisions. It never replaces responsible human leadership.

## Capabilities

- Strategic insights from participation, leadership, relationships, health, and outcomes
- Opportunity and risk detection with explainable evidence
- Early warning system (informational through critical)
- Scenario planning and probability-based forecasting
- Strategic recommendations (advisory only, human approval required)
- Decision Support Center with full audit trail
- Institutional Learning Engine from intervention outcomes
- Federation intelligence (aggregated best practices only)

## Purpose

**[IAS-M01]** The assistant becomes a **campaign strategist** — analyzing, planning, writing, and organizing while keeping humans in control of every consequential decision.

---

## Capabilities

**[IAS-M02]** Example queries:

- "Why is Benton County slowing?"
- "Find organizations working on food insecurity."
- "Build a volunteer plan."
- "Prepare tomorrow's agenda."
- "Write follow-up emails."
- "Analyze this legislation."
- "Show counties likely to flip."

---

## AI Context

**[IAS-M03]** The assistant understands: entire CRM · calendar · relationships · campaigns · organizations · goals · events · history · notes · documents · analytics · legislation · news · geography · volunteer history

**[IAS-M03a]** Context grounded in [Canonical Knowledge Kernel CKK-001] — Canon IDs cited in engineering output.

---

## AI Memory

**[IAS-M04]** Remembers: current projects · recent discussions · long-term goals · preferred workflows · decision history · campaign priorities

**[IAS-M04a]** Users may always inspect, correct, or clear remembered context.

---

## AI Guardrails

**[IAS-M05]** The AI must:

- Explain why it made a recommendation
- Cite data sources whenever possible
- Distinguish facts from predictions
- Never send emails, publish content, or modify campaign data without explicit approval
- Keep a complete audit log of recommendations and user actions

**[IAS-M05a]** Implements [KDG-M16] aggregation rules and privacy classification.

---

## Phase 7 Completion

**[IAS-M06]** When Phase 7 is complete, the platform evolves from information management into an intelligence platform that discovers opportunities, prevents problems, strengthens relationships, and accelerates informed decisions — without replacing human judgment.

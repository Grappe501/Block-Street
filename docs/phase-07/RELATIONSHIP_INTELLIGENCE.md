# Relationship Intelligence

**Document ID:** PHASE-007.5  
**Requirement:** RLI-001  
**Status:** Canonical  
**Phase:** 7 — Intelligence Layer

> Campaigns run on relationships. The platform should understand them.

**Builds on:** [Personal Relationship Network PRN-001](../phase-03/PERSONAL_RELATIONSHIP_NETWORK.md) · [Community Knowledge Graph NISS-M17](../phase-06/NETWORK_INTELLIGENCE_STRATEGY_SYSTEM.md) · [DAB-004 Relationship Graph](../volume-02/MASTER_DATA_DICTIONARY.md)  
**Live spec:** `data/registry/relationship-intelligence-engine.json` · **Build:** [RELATIONSHIP_INTELLIGENCE_ENGINE.md](RELATIONSHIP_INTELLIGENCE_ENGINE.md) · **API:** `/api/relationships`

---

## Purpose

**[RLI-M01]** Probably the most valuable Phase 7 capability — a **living relationship graph** that measures strength, health, influence, and engagement over time.

---

## Relationship Graph

**[RLI-M02]** Typed edges connecting participants, organizations, institutions, events, and roles — unified with [Community Knowledge Graph NISS-M17].

---

## Relationship Strength

**[RLI-M03]** Calculated from: meetings · emails · texts · calls · events · volunteer work · shared organizations · projects · recommendations · mutual contacts

---

## Relationship Health

**[RLI-M04]** States: Healthy · Growing · Weakening · Inactive · Conflict · Dormant

---

## Relationship Alerts

**[RLI-M05]** Examples:

- "You haven't spoken in 120 days."
- "This organization has become highly active."
- "County chair becoming disengaged."
- "Donor relationship cooling."

---

## Network Analysis

**[RLI-M06]** Find connectors · influencers · hidden leaders · isolated volunteers · bridge organizations

---

## Influence Mapping

**[RLI-M07]** Not title — **actual influence**. Who moves people? Who recruits? Who convinces? Who organizes? Who inspires?

**[RLI-M07a]** Influence scores are explainable and auditable — never opaque rankings.

---

## Deliverable

**[RLI-M08]** Living relationship graph with strength, health, alerts, network analysis, and influence mapping.

**Acceptance:** `AC-170`

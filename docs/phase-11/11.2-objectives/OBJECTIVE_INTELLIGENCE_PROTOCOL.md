# Objective Intelligence Protocol

**CAE-11.2-W6** · Contract `11.2-w6.1`

## Doctrine

Intelligence may observe, learn, compare, forecast, recommend, explain, and summarize.

Intelligence may never approve, assign authority, complete work, override governance, or change objectives.

Human judgment remains authoritative.

## Event Subscribers

Intelligence refreshes on: ObjectiveCreated, MissionCompleted, ReviewSubmitted, EvidenceAdded, OutcomeRecorded, RiskCreated, OwnerChanged, TimelineChanged.

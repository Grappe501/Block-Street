# Objective Success Doctrine

**Build:** 11.2 · **Wave:** W1

## Layer independence

| Layer complete | Does NOT imply |
|----------------|----------------|
| Task | Mission success |
| Mission | Objective success |
| Objective | Initiative success |

## Outcome categories

Objectives may finish as:

- Achieved
- Exceeded
- Partially Achieved
- Not Achieved
- Cancelled
- Replaced
- Continuing
- Unable to Measure

The platform encourages **honest reporting** rather than binary success/failure.

## Evidence requirement

Outcome classification requires evidence and Human review authority acceptance (W3).

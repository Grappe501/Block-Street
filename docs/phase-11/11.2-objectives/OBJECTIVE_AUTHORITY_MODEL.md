# Objective Authority Model

**Build:** 11.2 · **Wave:** W1

Each Objective has governed ownership roles following the Identity Trust model (Build 11.1):

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Executive Owner | Institutional legitimacy, outcome acceptance, escalation |
| Operational Owner | Day-to-day execution, reporting, review initiation |
| Review Authority | Conducts scheduled and exception reviews |
| Contributor | Performs work toward the Objective |
| Observer | Read access without execution authority |

Service identities and AI may not hold ownership. Ownership follows invitation and acceptance patterns from INI-001.

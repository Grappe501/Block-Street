# Objective Optimization Protocol

**Protocol ID:** CAE-11.2-W7 · **Constitution:** OBJ-OPT-001

## Principles

1. Every completed Objective produces institutional learning.
2. Failures are institutional assets — honest reporting over artificial success.
3. Recommendations never change execution automatically — Humans approve improvements.
4. Templates evolve; history is preserved; nothing is overwritten.

## Prohibited Actions

Optimization must never: approve Objectives, activate automation, delete history, rewrite governance, spend money, or auto-implement recommendations.

## Explainability

Every optimization answers: what changed, why, evidence, expected benefit, possible risk, confidence, and who should review.

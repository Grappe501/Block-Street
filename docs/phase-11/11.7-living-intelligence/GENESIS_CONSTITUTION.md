# Genesis Constitution (LIX-016)

**Protocol:** CAE-11.7-W16

## Governing Principle

> Institutions should outlive the individuals who build them.

## Genesis Package Must Include

Purpose, Mission, Vision, Values, Constitution, Governance, Identity, Founding principles, Historical amendments, Institutional DNA, Capabilities, Knowledge, and Provenance.

## Genesis May

- Export genesis packages as birth certificates
- Preserve institutional DNA independently of platforms
- Archive with immutable provenance
- Plan continuity and recover from disaster
- Bootstrap institutions from governed packages
- Migrate across vendors without captivity
- Verify authenticity and maintain historical timelines
- Support succession under Human stewardship

## Genesis May Not

- Rewrite institutional history
- Delete constitutional records
- Allow unverifiable provenance
- Bind permanently to one vendor
- Bypass governance during recovery
- Replace Human stewardship with automated constitutional authority
- Permit silent loss of institutional memory

## Human Stewardship

Constitutional preservation remains a Human responsibility. Automation assists; stewards decide.

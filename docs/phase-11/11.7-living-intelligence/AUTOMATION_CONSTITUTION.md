# Automation Constitution — CAE-11.7-W12

> **Authority is delegated intentionally. Automation is earned. Oversight never disappears.**

## May

Execute approved workflows · Request Human approval · Pause on failure · Retry with limits · Escalate to Human · Schedule authorized work · Run operational playbooks

## May Not

Execute unapproved high-risk workflows · Escalate authority automatically · Grant new permissions · Spend without approval · Delete audit history · Conceal failures · Continue after unrecoverable errors · Override Human intervention · Mutate canonical records outside contracts · Self-modifying automation

Every automation must declare purpose, institution, owner, authority, permissions, inputs, outputs, required approvals, failure behavior, recovery behavior, audit requirements, and retention. Default automation level remains **Level 1 — AI Assists**.

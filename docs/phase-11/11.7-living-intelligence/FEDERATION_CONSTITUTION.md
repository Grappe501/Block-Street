# Federation Constitution — CAE-11.7-W11

> **Federation without centralization. Collaboration without loss of sovereignty.**

## May

Voluntary participation · Explicit sharing · Contractual trust · Shared missions · Knowledge publish · Resource coordination · Coalition intelligence · Cross-institution AI · Federated search

## May Not

Merge institutional memories · Silent ownership transfer · Auto-share private · Permission inheritance · Administrative takeover · Unauthorized sync · Remove provenance · Centralized ownership · Cross-institution canonical mutation · Shadow organizations

Every institution declares identity, ownership, governance, jurisdiction, policies, permissions, data ownership, sharing rules, and trust relationships. Participation remains voluntary.

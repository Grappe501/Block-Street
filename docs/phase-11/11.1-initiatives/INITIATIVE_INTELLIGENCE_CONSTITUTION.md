# Initiative Intelligence Constitution

**Subsystem:** INI-INT-001

## Governing Principle

> The Intelligence Layer exists to increase Human wisdom—not automate institutional authority.

## Rules

1. **Advisory only** — no lifecycle mutations from intelligence or AI paths
2. **Explainability** — every recommendation answers why, based on what, how certain
3. **Dismissible** — feedback is logged; domain state unchanged
4. **Permission-first** — institution scope resolved before analysis
5. **No fabricated authority** — confidence bands are visible, not hidden scores
6. **Duplicate safety** — suggest comparison; never auto-merge or archive

## Prohibited AI Actions

- Approve · Assign ownership · Spend money · Activate · Close · Invite · Delete · Override governance

## Confidence Levels

`very_high` · `high` · `medium` · `low` · `speculative`

## Recommendation Shape

Every recommendation includes: title (EN/ES), why, confidence, evidence[], uncertainty_notes[], suggested_action, optional href, dismissible flag.

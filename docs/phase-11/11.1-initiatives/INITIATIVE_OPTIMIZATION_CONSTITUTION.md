# Initiative Optimization Constitution

**Build:** 11.1 · **Wave:** W7 · **Subsystem:** INI-OPT-001

## Human authority

Humans remain responsible for:

- Approving improvements
- Changing governance
- Publishing templates
- Changing workflows
- Accepting optimizations
- Updating policy

## The system may

Recommend · Summarize · Forecast · Compare · Detect · Estimate · Simulate · Teach · Prioritize

## The system may never

- Rewrite governance
- Reassign authority
- Activate automation
- Delete history
- Approve initiatives
- Spend money
- Invite people
- Modify institutional policy

## Explainability

Every optimization answers:

- What changed?
- Why?
- Evidence?
- Confidence?
- Expected benefit?
- Possible downside?
- Who should review?

## Feedback loop

Every recommendation receives: `accepted` · `rejected` · `modified` · `deferred` · `not_applicable`

Rejected recommendations teach the platform via `optimization/feedback-store.ts`.

## Certification

Wave 7 succeeds when institutional knowledge improves continuously, repeated mistakes decrease, templates mature, training becomes proactive, executives receive strategic guidance, simulations support decisions, organizational health is visible, every recommendation is explainable, and no optimization bypasses governance.

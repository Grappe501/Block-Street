# Initiative Closeout Constitution

**Build:** 11.1 · **Wave:** W1 · **Canonical source:** [01_CONSTITUTION.md](01_CONSTITUTION.md)

## Closeout Basis (Required)

Every initiative must define at least one:

- Objective completed
- Program period concluded
- Strategic priority changed
- Initiative replaced
- Initiative cancelled
- Funding ended
- Institutional authority withdrawn
- Emergency resolved
- Pilot evaluated
- Permanent operating function created

## Closing Questions

Every closing initiative must answer:

1. Why is it ending?
2. Who authorized closeout?
3. Were objectives achieved?
4. What work remains incomplete?
5. Which Humans are affected?
6. Which resources must be returned?
7. Which data must be retained or deleted?
8. Which communications are required?
9. Which outcomes were observed?
10. Which lessons were learned?
11. Should a successor initiative be created?
12. Should a playbook be produced?

## Completion Categories

```text
completed_as_planned · completed_with_adjustments · partially_completed
pilot_concluded · transitioned_to_continuous_operation · replaced_by_successor
cancelled_strategic · cancelled_resource · cancelled_safety_compliance
emergency_resolved · no_longer_necessary
```

## Cancellation Requirements

Authority, reason, effective date, unfinished obligations, Human impact, resource disposition, data retention, communications, lessons — all preserved in history (CON-017).

## Playbook Source

Completed initiatives may become templates after review and privacy redaction (Build 11.14).

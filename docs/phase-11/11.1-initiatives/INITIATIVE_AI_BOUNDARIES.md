# Initiative AI Boundaries

**Build:** 11.1 · **Wave:** W1 · **Canonical source:** [01_CONSTITUTION.md](01_CONSTITUTION.md)

**Requirement:** CAE-11.1-W1-CON-015

## AI May Assist

- Draft initiative charter
- Suggest initiative types
- Identify missing fields
- Recommend objectives
- Summarize risks
- Identify possible duplication
- Suggest structure

Advisory intelligence in **11.1-W6** operates within these boundaries.

## AI May Not

- Create an **active** initiative without Human action
- Approve an initiative
- Serve as initiative owner (executive, operational, or backup)
- Materially expand initiative scope
- Allocate high-risk resources
- Create institutional authority
- Cancel an initiative
- Declare successful completion

## Platform Flags

`PHASE_11_AI_AUTONOMOUS_ACTIONS_DISABLED` and `AI_CONSEQUENTIAL_ACTIONS_DISABLED` in `data/civic-action/cae_flags.json` reinforce this constitution.

## Acceptance Scenario 3

AI drafts charter → attempts self-ownership → system rejects → qualified Human reviews and accepts ownership → proceeds to review.

Enforcement: 11.1-W3 service validation, 11.1-W6 advisory-only mode.

## Relationship to Strategic Intelligence

Phase 10 strategic intelligence may recommend new initiatives, pause, review, consolidation — recommendations do not create or alter initiatives automatically.

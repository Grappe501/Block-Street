# Mission Traceability Standard

Operational missions must trace to:

- `program_id` and `objective_id` (minimum)
- `strategic_goal_id`, `pillar_id`, `mission_statement_id`, `vision_id` (resolved from Wave 1)

Use `explainMissionPurpose()` to answer why a mission exists.

Tasks inherit mission traceability through `mission_id` — tasks never exist without a mission.

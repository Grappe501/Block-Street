# CAE-11.6-W5 — Resource & Financial Stewardship Protocol

**Protocol ID:** `CAE-11.6-W5`  
**System:** OPS-001

## Constitutional Principle

> Resources exist to accomplish the Institution's mission. Assets are never collected simply to own them.

Every resource must answer: **Which Mission, Program, Project, or Strategic Objective does this support?**

## API Namespace

Resource stewardship APIs use **`/api/v1/resources`**, **`/api/v1/assets`**, **`/api/v1/inventory`**, **`/api/v1/facilities`**, **`/api/v1/budgets`**, **`/api/v1/expenses`**, **`/api/v1/reservations`**, **`/api/v1/purchases`**, **`/api/v1/maintenance`**.

## Stewardship Philosophy

Stewardship answers: what is it, who is responsible, where is it, who is using it, what condition is it in, what does it cost, what mission does it support, when does it need maintenance, when should it be replaced.

## Integration

- **W1** — Strategic objectives and budget planning
- **W2** — Mission assignments and evidence
- **W3** — Human availability and capacity
- **W4** — Organizational stewardship roles
- **W6 (future)** — Calendar and reservations

## AI Boundaries

AI may recommend allocation, procurement timing, inventory replenishment, and maintenance scheduling. AI may **not** purchase, dispose, or commit expenditures autonomously.

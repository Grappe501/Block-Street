# Skill and Competency Model

**Protocol:** CAE-11.12-W2

## Skill

Atomic capability within a knowledge domain.

## Competency

Demonstrated capability composed of skills. Lifecycle: `draft → defined → active → deprecated → archived`.

## CompetencyLevel

Graduated proficiency criteria within a competency.

## HumanCompetencyRecord

Tracks human demonstration and verification. Requires evidence — reading completion alone is insufficient (W1 doctrine).

## Assessment linkage

Assessments may bind to competency and/or course for demonstrated capability verification.

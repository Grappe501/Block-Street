# Knowledge Production Constitution

**Protocol ID:** CAE-11.12-W8 · **Contract:** KNW-PRD-001

## Production invariants

- No production certification without critical constitutional and security gates passing.
- Every certification result binds exact code, schema, contract, and configuration versions.
- Knowledge records remain recoverable from tested backups and event history.
- AI readiness certified independently from ordinary software functionality.
- Identity, authorization, evidence, credential, or institution-isolation failures block launch.
- Passed certification applies only to the evaluated release and environment.
- Every launch preserves a tested rollback path.
- Continuous verification continues after launch.

## No auto-deploy

The system may recommend launch readiness but must never auto-deploy. Humans approve pilot, limited production, and general availability.

## AI launch posture

At early launch: Tutor enabled with grounding; recommendations advisory; knowledge suggestions require Human review; competency verification Human only; certification award by authorized Human; autonomous canonical changes disabled.

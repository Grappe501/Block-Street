## Constitutional Doctrine

Knowledge is one of the Institution's most valuable assets.

Knowledge must be:

* discoverable
* teachable
* verifiable
* continuously improving
* preserved
* explainable
* reusable

Knowledge belongs to the Institution while respecting ownership, licensing, and privacy constraints established by policy.

---

## Primary Mission

The Knowledge Engine answers:

* What do we know?
* Why do we know it?
* How confident are we?
* Where did it come from?
* Who should learn it?
* When should it be updated?
* What knowledge is missing?

## Wave Architecture

### Wave 1 — Knowledge Constitution Protocol (CAE-11.12-W1)

**Purpose:** Define constitutional philosophy.

Topics: knowledge doctrine, institutional intelligence, truth & evidence, source attribution, knowledge ownership, knowledge lifecycle, learning philosophy, Human capability, AI boundaries, institutional wisdom.

**Deliverable:** Constitutional foundation of institutional learning.

---

### Wave 2 — Knowledge Canonical Data Model Protocol (CAE-11.12-W2)

**Purpose:** Define canonical entities.

Entities: Knowledge Article, Lesson, Course, Learning Path, Skill, Competency, Certification, Assessment, Research Note, Playbook, SOP, FAQ, Best Practice, Citation, Source, Knowledge Relationship, Learning Session, Tutor Conversation.

**Deliverable:** Complete canonical knowledge schema.

---

### Wave 3 — Knowledge Domain Services Protocol (CAE-11.12-W3)

**Purpose:** Implement business logic.

Services: Knowledge Service, Course Service, Learning Path Service, Competency Service, Assessment Service, Certification Service, Tutor Service, Recommendation Service, Knowledge Graph Service, Search Service, Version Service, Review Service.

**Deliverable:** Complete Knowledge Engine.

---

### Wave 4 — Learning Workbench & Human Experience Protocol (CAE-11.12-W4)

**Purpose:** Design learning experience.

Screens: My Learning, Knowledge Library, AI Tutor, Course Player, Learning Dashboard, Skills Profile, Certifications, Assessments, Research Workspace, Notes, Bookmarks, Recommendations.

**Goal:** Every Human knows what to learn, why it matters, how it applies, and what comes next.

---

### Wave 5 — Knowledge APIs, Events & Integration Protocol (CAE-11.12-W5)

**Purpose:** Integrate Knowledge with Mission Operations, Communications, Calendar, AI, Organizations, Analytics, Search, external LMS, libraries, and document systems. Event-driven.

---

### Wave 6 — Knowledge Intelligence & Adaptive Learning Protocol (CAE-11.12-W6)

**Purpose:** Adaptive learning, AI tutoring, prerequisite detection, competency forecasting, knowledge gap detection, personalized recommendations, citation analysis, research assistance, concept mapping, explainability. AI teaches — never replaces learning.

---

### Wave 7 — Knowledge Evolution & Institutional Wisdom Protocol (CAE-11.12-W7)

**Purpose:** Curriculum evolution, playbook evolution, competency evolution, institutional wisdom, research integration, continuous review, outdated knowledge detection, expert validation, knowledge stewardship, organizational learning.

---

### Wave 8 — Knowledge Production Readiness & Certification Protocol (CAE-11.12-W8)

**Purpose:** Constitutional review, citation validation, accessibility, multilingual certification, AI governance, curriculum review, assessment validation, security, disaster recovery, executive approval.

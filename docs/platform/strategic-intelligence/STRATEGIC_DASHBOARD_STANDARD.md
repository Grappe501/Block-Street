# Strategic Dashboard Standard

**System ID:** INT-002

## Strategic Dashboard

- Strategic opportunities count
- High risks count
- Leadership forecast
- Volunteer trend
- Community capacity
- Top recommendation
- Active warnings

## Executive Dashboard

- Top opportunities and risks
- Forecasts
- Strategic priorities
- Resource recommendations
- Pending decisions

Accessible tables and screen-reader summaries required.

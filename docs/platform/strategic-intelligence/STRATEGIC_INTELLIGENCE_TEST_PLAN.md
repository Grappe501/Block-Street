# Strategic Intelligence Test Plan

**System ID:** INT-002 · AC-199

## Acceptance Test (15 steps)

1. Platform aggregates participation, leadership, relationship, health, and outcome data
2. Strategic Intelligence identifies declining mentorship capacity
3. Early Warning System generates Concern alert with evidence
4. AI recommends mentor development cohort
5. Leaders review via Decision Support Center
6. Scenario compares with/without mentor recruitment
7. Leaders approve recommendation
8. Leadership cohort launched
9. Six months later mentorship capacity improves, warning resolved
10. Institutional Learning Engine records successful intervention
11. Executive dashboards show improved resilience
12. Federation identifies reusable best practice (aggregated only)
13. Decision history records recommendation, approval, implementation, outcome
14. AI updates future recommendations using documented learning
15. Full audit trail for insights, forecasts, recommendations, decisions

Run via **Phase 10 → Strategic Intelligence → Acceptance Demo**.

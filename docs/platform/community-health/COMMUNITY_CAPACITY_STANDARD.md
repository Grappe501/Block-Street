# Community Capacity Standard

**System ID:** CHD-001

## Capacity vs Activity

Capacity measures ability to accomplish **future** work:

- Active volunteers
- Available leaders
- Mentor availability percent
- Organizational readiness
- Collaboration index
- Training completion percent

Capacity differs from current activity — a community may be active today but lack depth for tomorrow.

API: `GET /api/v1/community-health/capacity`

# Community Health Test Plan

**System ID:** CHD-001 · AC-197

## Acceptance Test (15 steps)

1. Three community organizations begin using the platform in the same county
2. Volunteer participation, leadership, mentoring, and projects recorded over time
3. Community Health Profiles automatically generated
4. Health Index reflects participation, leadership, and volunteer capacity
5. Mentorship decline triggers early warning
6. AI recommends expanding mentor recruitment
7. New community project strengthens cross-organization collaboration
8. Relationship density and resilience scores improve
9. Executive dashboards show healthier organizational diversity
10. County reports compare year-over-year trends
11. Anonymous benchmarking against similar-sized communities
12. Geographic maps display civic capacity across county
13. Annual Community Health Report published (aggregated only)
14. Federation analytics use aggregated health metrics only
15. Full audit trail for calculations, recommendations, reports, benchmarks

Run via **Phase 10 → Community Health → Acceptance Demo**.

# Invitation Scope Policy

Invitations may propose institution membership, unit membership, workspace access, basic role, and onboarding — not direct high-risk authority.

## Safe default

Accepted invitation → canonical Human active → **provisional** institution membership → basic member role → verification pending → elevated roles require separate approval.

## Elevated roles

Invitation may *nominate* elevated roles; activation requires administrative gates, training, and certification.

Flag: `INVITATION_SCOPE_ENFORCEMENT_ENABLED`, `PROVISIONAL_MEMBERSHIP_DEFAULT`

Invariant: `ITL-W1-INV-010` — invitations cannot exceed inviter's authorized scope.

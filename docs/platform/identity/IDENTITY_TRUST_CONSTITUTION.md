# Identity Trust Constitution

**ITF-001 / ITL-W1-001**

## Governing Rule

> No invitation, no account. No accountable sponsor, no invitation. No canonical human identity, no platform access.

## Wave 1 Commitments

1. **One Entrance** — Invitation only
2. **One Human Identity** — One Global Human ID per person
3. **Public Human Identity** — Real names or approved aliases
4. **Permanent Invitation Lineage** — Append-only after acceptance
5. **Sponsor Accountability** — Limited claim of reasonable belief

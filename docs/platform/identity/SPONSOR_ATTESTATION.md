# Sponsor Attestation

Required acknowledgments before sending an invitation:

**Primary**

> I personally know this individual, or I have directly verified enough information to reasonably believe that this invitation is going to the real human identified here. I understand that this invitation will remain permanently associated with my account.

**Secondary**

> I am responsible for the integrity of this invitation. I am not guaranteeing the person's future behavior, opinions, or performance.

Stored in `SponsorAttestation` with version from `wave1_policy.json`.

Flag: `SPONSOR_ATTESTATION_REQUIRED`

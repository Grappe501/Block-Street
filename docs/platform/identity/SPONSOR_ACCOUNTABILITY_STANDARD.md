# Sponsor Accountability Standard (ITL-SPN-001)

## Sponsor claim

> I personally know this individual, or I have directly verified enough to reasonably believe this invitation goes to the real human identified here.

## Sponsor does not guarantee

Future behavior, opinions, job performance, or perpetual control.

## After acceptance

Sponsor cannot delete the human, read private activity, alter public identity, or inherit permissions. Lineage is permanent; ownership is not.

## Privilege states

`eligible`, `limited`, `paused`, `under_review`, `suspended`, `revoked`

## Default Wave 1 limits

| Level | Active invitations |
|-------|-------------------|
| New sponsored member | 0 |
| Verified member | 3 |
| Trusted member | 10 |
| Institution leader | Configured |

## Education

Sponsor orientation is versioned and required before invitation privileges.

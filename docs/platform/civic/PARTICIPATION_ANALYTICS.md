# Participation Analytics

**System ID:** CIV-001

Institutional analytics: retention, volunteer growth, leadership growth, participation diversity, engagement gaps.

Federation analytics: aggregate participation index, anonymous trends, community growth—never institution-specific private data without permission.

AI insights remain advisory: opportunities, interventions, forecasts, burnout detection. AI never judges civic worth.

**API:** `GET /api/v1/civic/insights` · `GET /api/v1/civic/engagement`

# Identity Trust Privacy

**System ID:** ITF-001

## Public

Other users see:

- Public name (or approved known alias)
- Trust badge: Sponsored Member, Verified Human, Trusted, or Leader

## Private

Other users do **not** see:

- Who verified you
- How you verified
- Verification notes
- Legal name (unless you choose to share)

## Verification Records

Accessible only to the subject, institution leaders, and authorized administrators.

## Real Name Policy

Default identity is the name you are publicly known by — not anonymous handles. Known aliases (authors, pastors, musicians, community figures) may be approved when they represent a real public identity.

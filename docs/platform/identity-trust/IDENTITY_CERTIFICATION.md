# Identity Certification

**ITL-CERT-001**

## Pre-Launch Verification

14 certification checks before launch:

1. No anonymous accounts
2. Invitation only
3. Sponsored lineage
4. Permanent sponsor agreement
5. Global Human ID on all identities
6. Immutable identity history
7. Cross-institution identity without shared permissions
8. Verification privacy
9. Public name policy
10. Identity review process
11. Appeals process
12. Duplicate detection
13. AI advisory only
14. Complete audit trail

# Identity Governance

**ITL-GOV-001**

## Identity Review Board

Reviews for suspected fake identities. Outcomes: verified, needs more evidence, restricted, removed.

## Appeals

Subjects may appeal review outcomes. Human resolver required.

## Policies

Duplicate identities, identity correction, known aliases, institution transfers, identity retirement.

## APIs

- `GET/POST /api/v1/identity-trust/reviews`
- `GET/POST /api/v1/identity-trust/appeals`

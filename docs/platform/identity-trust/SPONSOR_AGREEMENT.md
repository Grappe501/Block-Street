# Sponsor Agreement

**System ID:** ITF-001

## Responsibility

> "I reasonably believe this person is who they claim to be."

Not:

> "I guarantee every future action."

## Agreement Text

Before sending an invitation, sponsors must accept:

> I personally know this individual or have verified their identity sufficiently to believe they are who they claim to be.

## Permanent Record

Every invitation permanently records:

- Who created it
- When
- Why
- Institution
- Organization
- Intended role

The inviter is permanently linked to the invited member in audit lineage.

## Revocation

Sponsors do **not** own the invited account. If a sponsor leaves, invited members remain.

## Bad Actor Detection

If multiple flagged accounts originate from one sponsor, invite privileges may be paused pending administrative investigation.

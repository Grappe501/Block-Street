# Identity Trust Constitution

**ITF-001 / ITL-001**

## Foundational Principle

The Identity Trust Layer replaces traditional account registration. Every module depends upon verified human identity.

## Seven Constitutional Principles

1. One Human · One Identity · One Accountability Chain
2. There is only ONE way to enter: Invitation
3. Every invitation creates permanent lineage
4. Identity is built through trust, not passwords
5. Trust grows over time — it is never purchased
6. Verification protects the community — it does not exclude people
7. Every important action is attributable to a real human

## AI Constraints

AI may detect, recommend, and explain. AI may **never** approve, deny, or remove identities.

## Integration

Authentication, organizations, leadership, participation, relationships, messaging, analytics, federation — all inherit ITL assurance.

# Trust Levels

**System ID:** ITF-001

## Levels

### Level 0 — Pending Invitation
Invitation created but not yet accepted.

### Level 1 — Sponsored
One known sponsor. Default after invitation acceptance. **0 invites.**

### Level 2 — Verified
At least one independent confirmation. **0 invites** until trusted.

### Level 3 — Trusted
Two independent verifications plus sustained healthy participation (30+ days). **5 invites.**

### Level 4 — Institution Leader
Approved by institution. **Unlimited invites** (configurable).

## Not a Social Score

The identity trust score is simply: Pending → Sponsored → Verified → Trusted → Leader. Nothing more.

## Public Display

Profiles show badges only:

- Sponsored Member
- Verified Human
- Trusted
- Leader

Never numeric scores.

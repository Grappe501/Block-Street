# Verification Standard

**System ID:** ITF-001

## Methods

- Known personally
- Worked together
- Organization leader
- Government ID (optional — not required for ordinary participation)
- Institution verification
- Video verification
- Community verification

## Independent Verification

Two **independent** verifications (from different verifiers, not the primary sponsor) unlock Trusted status progression.

One verification keeps the member at Sponsored or Verified depending on independence.

## Verification Record (Private)

```text
Verifier → Relationship → Date → Method → Confidence
```

Verification records are **never** shown to other users.

## Review Instead of Removal

No verification after a defined review period triggers **identity review**, not automatic account removal.

# Identity Intelligence

**ITL-INT-001**

## Detection

Duplicate identities, fraud rings, mass invitations, bot behavior, dormant identities, trust anomalies.

## AI Integration (Advisory Only)

AI may recommend verification and identity reviews. AI **cannot** approve or remove identities.

## APIs

- `GET /api/v1/identity-trust/analytics?mode=scan`
- `POST /api/v1/identity-trust/analytics` (advisory Q&A)

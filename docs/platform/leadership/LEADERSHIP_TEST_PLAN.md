# Leadership Test Plan

**System ID:** LDR-001 · AC-195

## Acceptance Test (16 steps)

1. Volunteer consistently participates
2. Verified LeadershipEvidence for projects and mentoring
3. System identifies Emerging Leader (advisory)
4. Mentor assigned
5. Development plan generated
6. Joins county leadership cohort
7. Leads community meeting
8. Competencies increase from evidence
9. Leadership Score with transparent breakdown
10. Team Leader opportunity eligibility
11. Executive dashboard reflects pipeline health
12. Succession plan with volunteer as candidate
13. AI recommends training (no appointments)
14. Privacy: public milestones, private evaluations
15. Analytics show improved mentor capacity
16. Full audit trail

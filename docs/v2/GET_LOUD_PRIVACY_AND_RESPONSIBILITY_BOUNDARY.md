# Get Loud Privacy and Responsibility Boundary

Block-Street must never:

- Embed the Jotform in an iframe
- Proxy submissions or recreate form fields
- Store DL numbers, SSN digits, signatures, or eligibility answers
- Claim applicant completed registration or county acceptance

Permitted workflow data: resource offered, external link opened (aggregate), verification reminder requested.

Sensitive-data warning required before opening external form.

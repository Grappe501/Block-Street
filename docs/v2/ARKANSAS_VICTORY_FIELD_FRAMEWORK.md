# Arkansas Victory Field Framework

**Altitude:** 30,000 feet  
**Status:** ingested into Field Plan seed  
**Machine twin:** `data/field-plan/victory-field-framework.json`  
**Role content:** `data/field-plan/position-content.json`

## Doctrine

> Every campaign event must leave behind more volunteers, more leaders, more voter registrations, more neighborhood teams, and stronger local infrastructure than existed before the event.

A crowd is good. Permanent organization is the objective.

## Catalyst

Campaign schedules an event → activates local event committee + central teams → timeline, communications, field, media, Power of Five, follow-up.

## Local event committee (30K ft)

Event Lead · Social Media Lead · Canvassing Lead · Volunteer Recruitment Lead · Community/Social Lead · Voter Registration Lead · Media Lead · Logistics Lead

Media and Logistics are tracked in the Field Plan framework first; CIWS functional role cards stay lean until drill-down adds them deliberately.

## Central activation (auto)

Field Director, Volunteer Management, Central Social, Graphics, Email, Phone/Text, Postcard, Young Adult, County Strike Team, Communications/Press, Data, Voter Registration Director, Fundraising, Operations.

## Thirteen phases

1. Event Activation  
2. Local Intelligence  
3. Communications Launch (three waves)  
4. Field Operation (doors, phone, text, postcards)  
5. Earned Media (incl. newspaper/radio two weeks out where budget allows)  
6. Event Experience Funnel → Power of Five  
7. Power of Five Launch  
8. Volunteer Leadership Ladder  
9. Voter Registration Operation  
10. Community Partnerships  
11. October Strike Team Saturdays  
12. Election Operations (every poll covered)  
13. Post-Event Continuity (24h / 1 week / 1 month)

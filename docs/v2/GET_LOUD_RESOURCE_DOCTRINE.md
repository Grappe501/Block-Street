# Get Loud Resource Doctrine

**Build:** BS-GET-LOUD-VOTER-REGISTRATION-1.0

Block-Street educates and refers. Get Loud Arkansas operates the external assistance tool. The county clerk determines acceptance.

## Relationship

Use **Independent Arkansas civic-engagement resource** — not partner, not owned.

## Registry

Canonical contract: `data/registry/external-civic-resources.json`

## Prohibited

- Embed, proxy, prefill, or store Jotform submissions
- Claim registration completion or county acceptance
- Scatter raw URLs across components

## QDL posture

Compact card on Home Explore; full explanation one click deeper; prominent on Registration workbenches only.

# North Star

## By Election Season

An Arkansas student or young adult should be able to:

1. **Find their home** — campus hub if in school, county hub if not
2. **Sign up** — name, contact, interests, affiliation
3. **Get their own network** — personal share link + QR code
4. **Recruit their people** — relational organizing from day one
5. **Join or start committees** — local and cross-campus/county
6. **See events & outreach** — campus, county, and statewide
7. **Connect across Arkansas** — find students at other schools
8. **Shape collective voice** — surveys, delegates, campus votes — **they** decide who and what to support

The platform never answers *"who should we support?"*

It answers *"how do we organize to decide that together?"*

## Success Metrics (Qualitative)

| Metric | Question |
|--------|----------|
| Relationships | How many trusted connections are we building? |
| Campus coverage | How many Arkansas schools have active hubs? |
| County coverage | How many of 75 counties have active youth signups? |
| Committee formation | How many local + cross-campus committees exist? |
| Event participation | How many students show up to organized events? |
| Leader development | How many participants become active organizers? |

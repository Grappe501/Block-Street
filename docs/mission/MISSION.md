# Mission

## One Sentence

**Give Arkansas college students and young adults one shared organizing home — where every campus builds its own voice, every county welcomes those not in school, and student energy becomes real civic power.**

## What We Are Building

A **nonpartisan student and youth organizing platform** for Arkansas.

The purpose is **not** to tell students who to support, what party to join, or what issues to prioritize.

The purpose **is** to give young Arkansans the tools, structure, and shared space to organize themselves.

## Who It Serves

| Audience | Organizing Home |
|----------|-----------------|
| College & university students | Campus / school hub |
| Trade school & junior college students | Campus / school hub |
| Future participating high schools | Self-registering school hub |
| Young adults not currently in school | County hub (75 Arkansas counties) |

## What It Enables

- Recruit signups on campus and in county
- Communicate across campuses and counties
- Form committees (local + cross-campus/statewide)
- Coordinate outreach and events
- Build leadership organically
- Develop a collective civic voice heading into the next election

## What It Never Does

- Endorse candidates or parties
- Push preset issue positions
- Replace student decision-making with platform direction
- Rank organizers solely by recruit count

- *Student-led. Campus-rooted. County-connected. Nonpartisan by design.*
- *We provide the tools. Students choose the direction.*

# Audience Entry Paths

## Two Primary Paths

Every visitor chooses one path at signup. Both paths lead to the same platform — different organizing home.

---

## Path 1: I'm Connected to a School

**For:** College students, university students, trade school students, junior college students, and (future) high school participants.

### What They Get

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Campus hub | Landing page for their school |
| Campus committees | Local + cross-campus committees |
| Campus events | School-specific calendar |
| Campus leadership | Student reps for their school |
| Campus outreach | Recruitment tools scoped to campus |
| Personal network board | Share link + QR + their recruited network |

### School Types Supported

- 4-year universities & colleges
- Trade / technical schools
- Junior colleges
- High schools (future — self-registration workflow)

### Founding Leadership Council (Initial Anchors)

These five campuses sit on the initial leadership council — peers, not bosses:

1. University of Arkansas — Fayetteville
2. Ouachita Baptist University
3. Philander Smith University
4. University of Central Arkansas
5. University of Arkansas at Pine Bluff

All other Arkansas schools are equal participants from day one.

---

## Path 2: I'm Not Currently in School

**For:** Young adults aged out of or not enrolled in an educational institution.

### What They Get

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| County hub | Catch-all landing for their Arkansas county |
| County committees | Local youth organizing committees |
| County events | County-scoped calendar |
| County leadership | County youth reps |
| County outreach | Recruitment within the county |
| Personal network board | Share link + QR + their recruited network |

### Coverage

All **75 Arkansas counties** each get a dedicated hub:

- Pulaski County → `/county/pulaski`
- Faulkner County → `/county/faulkner`
- ... (see [ARKANSAS-75-COUNTIES.md](../counties/ARKANSAS-75-COUNTIES.md))

---

## Shared Across Both Paths

- Personal profile
- Share link (`/s/username`)
- QR code for recruitment
- Network board (people invited, growth, activity)
- Interest tags: leadership, events, outreach, communications, committees, voter education, issue organizing
- Ability to join statewide committees
- Nonpartisan platform rules

## Signup Flow (Planned)

```
Landing → Choose Path → Select Campus OR County → Profile → Interests → Your Network Board
```
